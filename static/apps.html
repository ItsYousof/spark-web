    Youtube

    YouTube is the world's largest video-sharing platform, allowing users to watch, upload, and share videos on a wide range of topics. From music and tutorials to vlogs and documentaries, YouTube offers a vast library of content, making it a go-to destination for entertainment.

    Tiktok

    TikTok is a popular social media platform for short-form videos, where users can create and share content ranging from lip-syncing to comedy sketches and dance challenges. With its engaging and easy-to-use interface, TikTok has become a global hub for creativity.

    Twitch

    Twitch is a leading live streaming platform primarily focused on gaming, but also featuring content related to music, art, and more. With a vibrant community of streamers and viewers, Twitch offers real-time interaction, allowing audiences to engage with their favorite creators.

    Google

    Google is a multinational technology company known for its search engine, Google Search, which provides access to vast amounts of information online. Beyond search, Google offers a range of products and services, including email (Gmail), cloud storage (Google Drive), ect.

    Discord

    Discord is a popular communication platform designed for communities, gamers, and friends to chat, voice call, and share media in real-time. With features like servers, channels, and customizable roles, Discord provides a versatile and engaging environment for people to connect with each other.

    File Loader

    This type of app would allow users to upload an HTML file, and upon clicking a "load" button, the app would display the content of the HTML file within the app's interface. This could be a useful tool for previewing and editing HTML files without needing to open them in a web browser. This app can bypass blocker too!

    Snapchat

    Snapchat is a multimedia messaging app where users can send photos and videos, known as "Snaps," that disappear after being viewed. With features like filters, lenses, and stories, Snapchat offers a fun and creative way for users to communicate and share moments with friends and followers.
